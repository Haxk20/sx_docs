```
/data/system
/data/system_ce
/data/system_de
/data/user
/data/user_ce
/data/user_de
/data/vendor
/data/vendor_ce
/data/vendor_de
```

`CE` stands for credential-encrypted.

See https://android-review.googlesource.com/c/platform/external/selinux/+/962651
